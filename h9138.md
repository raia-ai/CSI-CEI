# User defined material data
## Data Input
Description | Enter the material description.  
---|---  
Cast component | Select _Yes_ for cast component. 
* For EN 13445 - see [Cast Component](<h0298.htm#0700>).   
Design strength: ATM | Design strength for the shell or head material at room temperature. 
* For PD 5500 - see [Design Strengths](<h0098.htm#0300>). 
* For ASME Div.1 - see [Allowable Stresses](<h0198.htm#0100>). 
* For ASME Div.2 - see [Allowable Stresses](<h0598.htm#0100>).   
Design strength: DT | Design strength for the shell or head material at the design temperature. 
* For PD 5500 - see [Design Strengths](<h0098.htm#0300>). 
* For ASME Div.1 - see [Allowable Stresses](<h0198.htm#0100>). 
* For ASME Div.2 - see [Allowable Stresses](<h0598.htm#0100>).   
Yield: ATM | The specified minimum yield strength _R p 0.2_ for the material at room temperature. 
* For PD 5500 - see [Yield Strength](<h0098.htm#0400>). 
* For ASME Div.1 - see [Yield Strength](<h0198.htm#0800>). 
* For ASME Div.2 - see [Yield Strength](<h0598.htm#0800>). 
* For EN 13445 - see [Yield strength and Tensile strength](<h0298.htm#0800>).   
Yield: ATM \(1.0%\) | The specified minimum yield strength _R p 1.0_ for the material at room temperature. 
* For EN 13445 - see [Yield strength and Tensile strength](<h0298.htm#0800>).   
Yield: DT | The yield strength _R p 0.2/T_ for the material at the design temperature. 
* For PD 5500 - see [Yield Strength](<h0098.htm#0400>). 
* For ASME Div.1 - see [Yield Strength](<h0198.htm#0800>). 
* For ASME Div.2 - see [Yield Strength](<h0598.htm#0800>). 
* For EN 13445 - see [Yield strength and Tensile strength](<h0298.htm#0800>).   
Yield: DT \(1%\) | The yield strength _R p 1.0/T_ for the material at the design temperature. 
* For EN 13445 - see [Yield strength and Tensile strength](<h0298.htm#0800>).   
Min tensile strength | The minimum tensile strength _R m_ for the material at room temperature. 
* For PD 5500 - see [Minimum Tensile Strength](<h0098.htm#0400>). 
* For ASME Div.1 - see [Minimum Tensile Strength](<h0198.htm#0800>). 
* For ASME Div.2 - see [Minimum Tensile Strength](<h0598.htm#0800>). 
* For EN 13445 - see [Yield strength and Tensile strength](<h0298.htm#0800>).   
Min tensile strength: DT | The minimum tensile strength _R_ m/T for the material at the design temperature. 
* For PD 5500 - see [Minimum Tensile Strength](<h0098.htm#0400>). 
* For ASME Div.1 - see [Minimum Tensile Strength](<h0198.htm#0800>). 
* For ASME Div.2 - see [Minimum Tensile Strength](<h0598.htm#0800>). 
* For EN 13445 - see [Yield strength and Tensile strength](<h0298.htm#0800>).   
Yield factor s | The value of factor _s_ for the material.  
Elastic mod: ATM | The elastic modulus for the material at room temperature.  
Elastic mod: DT | The elastic modulus for the material at the design temperature.  
Expansion coeff \(xE6\) | The Expansion coefficient of the material at the design temperature.  
Poissons ratio | Poissons ratio of the material.
