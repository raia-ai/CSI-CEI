# Saddle Types
## Plain Saddle
Standard saddle support, typically extending over about one third of the circumference of the shell.
![](..\\img\\saddle.png)
## Internal and External Rings
Standard saddle support with a stiffening ring fitted inside or outside the shell, in the plane of the saddle.
![](..\\img\\sadring3.png)
## Internal and External Adjacent Rings
Standard saddle support with stiffening rings fitted inside or outside the shell, either side of each saddle, where _y_ is the _Distance between rings_.
![](..\\img\\sadring2.png)
## Ring Support
Vessel supported on external rings.
![](..\\img\\sadring.png)
