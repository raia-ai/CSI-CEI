# Item Orientation
To facilitate ease of entry of component locations there are three types of equipment orientation available:-
Horizontal - datum left | The datum point is typically at the _left most_ tangent line and locations increase to the right.  
---|---  
Vertical - datum low | The datum point is typically at the _lowest_ tangent line, or base plate and locations increase vertically.  
Horizontal - datum right | The datum point is typically at the _right most_ tangent line and locations increase to the left.
