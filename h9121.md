# Tray data
## Data Input
Description | Enter the tray description.  
---|---  
Type | Tray weight factor, directly proportional to the weight of the tray decks. A factor of 4.0 will give a realistic approximation for valve trays, 3.5 for sieve trays.  
Quantity | Quantity of trays in the section.  
## Additional Information
A tray can only be added to a vessel that has been specified as _Vertical_ in [Item orientation](<h9303.htm>) and have [Base Plate data](<h9120.htm>) defined in the model. NOTE: The tray is assumed to be made from Stainless steel with a specific gravity of 8.0.
## Error Messages
See also [General component error messages](<h9100.htm#1000>).
Diameter | The _Tray OD_ must be less than the attached _Shell ID_.
